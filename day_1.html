FOOD PLAN
SEPTEMBER 16 / DAY 1

  1. BREAKFAST: Lugaw with egg

    • CALORIES CONTENT
      1. Calories: 240
      2. Carbs: 3g
      3. Fat: 8g
      4. Protein: 6g
    • NUTRITIONAL ADVANTAGES AND BENEFITS
      1. Lugaw's simple ingredients make it easy to digest,
        but it benefits you from the inside of the stomach.
      2. Lugaw could provide an easy-to-digest source of energy by
        cooking rice, which is abundant in carbs. The high water
        content of lugaw can increase an individual's overall fluid
        consumption throughout the day, hence preventing dehydration.

  2. LUNCH: Sisig with 2 rice

    • CALORIES CONTENT
      1. 780g
    • NUTRITIONAL ADVANTAGES / BENEFIT
      1. Sisig is a rich source of protein, with each meal having around
        30-40 grams. Protein is necessary for tissue development and
        repair, and it can also make you feel full after eating.
      2. High in vitamins and minerals (68.6%/cal) and a strong source of
        Vitamin C and Manganese.

  3. DINNER: 2 apples

    • CALORIES CONTENT
      1. 317g
    • NUTRITIONAL ADVANTAGES / BENEFIT
      1. Apples are high in nutrients such as fiber, vitamin C, and
        antioxidants, which can promote healthy digestion, cognitive
        health, and weight management. There is evidence that apples
        can protect against certain serious illnesses such as cancer,
        heart disease, and type 2 diabetes.
